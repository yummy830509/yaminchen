Coffee, Sea and Sun

The U.S.A,
where people are full of energy.

I was so obsessed with the sun, the sea and the people here in California.

I am a girl who is not able to live without the sea.
Here - California, a place totally meets my need.

Besides, I went to graceful Seattle and overlooked the whole city
where romatic feelings spread all over the town.

Though I have just been here for two weeks.
The passion and hospitality of people here have impressed me much.
They gave me something worth cherishing.

The U.S.A, where I was refreshed and renewed.
