Coffee, Sea and Sun

There were many fascinating and interesting student clubs in Taipei First Girls' High.
Due to the curiosity of the magic and mysterious biology sphere. I had determined Life Science Reaserch Group among them then had learnt not only considerable crucial biologic conceptions but also tips of efficient team work.

The group is cooperated with students of Taipei Municipal Jianguo High School. We arrange a series of biological courses. Every member of the group is demanded to collect the information about the aspect he is interested in. After thinking, understanding and organizing, we take turns to share the content and the conceptions with other else then discuss the misunderstandings or different kinds of views of the issues.
Sometimes, we cooperate with biology teachers and invite friends out of the group to join our easy but amazing little experiments.

I had met a lot of versatile friends in Life Science Research Group which had ignited my primary passion to the world of Science. We still share our points of view of every cotroversial issue so far.
