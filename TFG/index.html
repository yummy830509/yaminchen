Coffee, Sea and Sun

Taipei First Girls' High is the place where I was enlighted. I have met a group of adorable girls I cherish most. Every little girl here was full of cleverness but humble. We enjoyed those joyful days, shared either tears or smiles, did something foolish together.

We learnt, We Jump, We Love. We improved our volleyball skills together in order to show our best team work during the contest. We learnt not only knowledge but also methods of making desserts from the class together. We celebrated every important days and created irreplaceable memories together.

We went through the challengable General Scholastic Ability Test. Four years later,
we made a pack to come back and celebrate our graduation.
