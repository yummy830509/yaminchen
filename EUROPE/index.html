Coffee, Sea and Sun

Europe,
an ancient continent on the earth,
has been through many cultures and dynasties.

I passed by the city center of Norway, and smelled the cold air I have never felt.
The snow fell on the surface of the frozen lake. "Maybe heaven is just like that." I thought.

I saw valuable relics which made me gasp in admiration in Pergamon museum.
Stood on the ground that Friedrich I had stood.
Imaged World War I and II beside Berlin Wall.
I really like Berlin, the city that makes me feel the trace of history.

Not pictures on the magazines, I finally saw Stonehenge with my own eye in England
and the excitement was beyond description.

Europe, an incredible history book.
