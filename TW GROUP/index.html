Coffee, Sea and Sun

Taiwanese Students' Affairs Group which is based on taiwanese students studying in
Tsinghua University has held lots kinds of student activities.
We make new taiwanese freshmen be close to each other and be familiar with senior schoolmates, deal with life problems together and discuss difficulties of schoolworks.

We hold not only annual camp for taiwanese high school students in order to introduce the life in Tsinghua University to them, but also Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan carnival annually.
At Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan carnival, We explain traditional cultures which are distinctive between mainland China and Taiwan by planning some interactive games, share tricky snacks with students from everywhere and tell some fascinating stories of the cities in Taiwan. Because of the carnival, many misunderstandings fade away and that's all my pleasure to get a few of adorable new friends in Tsinghua.

Though the schoolworks in Tsinghua was very challengable. Taiwanese Students' Affairs Group had brought me a lot of invincible memories. No matter how many years I have been graduated from Tsinghua, they are always irreplaceable.
