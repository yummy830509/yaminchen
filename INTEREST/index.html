Coffee, Sea and Sun

I am so obsessed with every different kind of Japanese novels and I appreciate every distinctive style of Japanese author. From ancient traditional Japanese culture 《人間失格》 to prevalent mystery novels 《告白》, I can easily fall into the stories they made and learn something important and useful to my life eventually. The more novels done by Japanese author I have read, the better I like Japanese succinct writting style.吉田修一,百田尚樹, 松本清張, 島田荘司, 太宰治, 奥田英朗,etc. They all created attractive beginning and powerful ending that I can't help but reading them in one sitting.

Coffee is a deep research.
It's the best moment that a book comes with a cup of coffee.
So, I like to digging the secret of a cup of delicate coffee. Including the method of doing an amazing cup of coffee, the tempurature and the humidity the best coffee beans grew under, the difference between every kind of coffee bean...
Only when you taste it carefully, you can find the key to the happiness.

Also, I like to experience how other people live every day. so I spent much time wandering in the city I have never been before. I lodged in backpacker's lodge and made many friends from totally different country. Someone comes from the country where snows three-forths of a year. Someone just comes from the tropical country countless unbelievable insects live in.
I like to try the food I have never tasted.
I like to feel the smells of every city I have never lived.

I have lots kinds of interes because I am curious about many little things in this world.
When I notice the changing of the world, I feel myself exist.
